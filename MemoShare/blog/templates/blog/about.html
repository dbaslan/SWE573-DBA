What is MemoShare?

MemoShare is an online platform for sharing your memories with people from around the world. Happy to sad, small to life-changing, you can share any stories you have with the world!

How do I use MemoShare?

You can create an account right now, and start making posts or reading what others have written!

What else can I do?

You can like memories, comment on them, or follow users so you will get to read all their posts!

Do I have to pay?

Nope! MemoShare is completely free of charge!

Can I read a few posts?

Sure! Go ahead and read a random post here!
